Analysis of Citi_Bike_NY_Story 

1. Rush hour(8:00 and 17:00-18:00) seems to be the peak hours for riders.

2. In the summer, more people ride bikes during the evening rush hour, but in the winter, more people ride bikes during the morning rush hour.

3. September 2019 had the lowest number of records at 49259, while April 2020 had the lowest number of records at 9273.

4. In May 2020, the number of records grew by 170% from April. This is the greatest increase because April had the lowest number of records.
